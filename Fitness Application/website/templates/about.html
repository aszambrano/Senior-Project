Welcome to Z-Fit!

  • Our goal at Z-Fit is to provide its users with a comprehensive list of resistance training exercises that are catered to your preference. All you need to do is create an account to fully access Z-Fit's features. Once an account is made, you will have full access to the apps features. These features include the following:


    • Exercise Glossary

  • The main feature of Z-Fit is the comprehensive list of resistance training exercises. It is a list of many exercises that have all been coveniently compiled in one place and is updated regularly.


    • Workout Journal

  • The last feature on Z-Fit is access to a workout journal for all of our users. Once logged onto the app, you can track your workouts by entering in the specific exercises you did that day and then saving it. This will allow for users to see their progress in their fitness journey!
